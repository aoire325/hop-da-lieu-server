BATTLE

HBDC

High School Best Dance Crew (HBDC) is the largest dance battle competition for high school students in Northern Vietnam. Entering its 6th season with the theme “ACE” (The Trump Card), HBDC aims to promote a deep appreciation for this street art form among future generations, attracting more than 25 dance crews, 3,000 participants, and 10,000 attendees. Through a variety of event formats, HBDC has provided a dynamic platform for sharing and developing dance skills, promoting the Vietnamese Hip-hop community. Within the competition, I participated as a dancer - member of Nguyen Hue Dance Club (NDC).

Choreography

HBDC

Choreography is an act of gathering and organizing movements into order and pattern. A dance choreography, specifically, embraces musicality and creativity. As a member of Nguyen Hue Dance Club (NDC), I have prior experiences with choreography.

FPTU HOLA SHOWCASE

FPTU Hola Showcase is a choreography competition organized by FPT University for highschool students in Nothern Vietnam. Performing before nearly 700 people on the final night, NDC was awarded “Style” prize.
