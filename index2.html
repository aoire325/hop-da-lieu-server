WAACKING

Waacking is a street dance style distinguished by its rotational arm movements, posing, and emphasis on expressiveness. Typically performed in disco music, the style allows me the freedom to embody drama and character through diverse modes of self-expression. With my favorite crop tops and powder blushes on, I couldn’t care less about the excess fat in my upper arms as music guides my body, embracing the personal story - the drama - each character holds. Be it an undefeated champion, a heartbroken lover, or a passionate admirer, Waacking, to me, feels like an escape from life to take on other personas that are brimming with emotion and creativity.

BATTLE

A dance battle is a dynamic showcase where dancers take turns on the dance floor to exhibit their skills within a designated set of rules. Connecting with a passionate network of young individuals—young men confidently wearing dresses, young women fearlessly performing head spins—I, too, am inspired to represent Waacking with confidence and drama. Sad, mad, angsty, and euphoric, every battle that I take on is a chance for me to portray my honest self through multiple layers of emotions. Rolling dramatically in chorus and letting movement flow on beat, I learn more about myself and from others. In the community, there is “No standing, just dancing”.

HBDC

High School Best Dance Crew (HBDC) is the largest dance battle competition for high school students in Northern Vietnam. Entering its 6th season with the theme “ACE” (The Trump Card), HBDC aims to promote a deep appreciation for this street art form among future generations, attracting thousands of attendees. Within the competition, I participated as a dancer - member of Nguyen Hue Dance Club (NDC). It was through a range of HBDC events that I have had the opportunity to embrace and showcase my full potential as a Waacker, a partner, and a leader.

CHOREOGRAPHY

Choreography is the art of gathering and organizing movements into order and pattern. A dance choreography, specifically, embraces musicality and creativity. To me, the articulation of movements closely incorporated with the flow of music serves as a channel for exploration. Elaborating on different layers of a beat, especially in disco music, I found myself gradually transforming into characters within each song, allowing its meaning to inspire body movement. It is the meaningful connection that awakens emotions deep within, unleashing ideas and self-expression.
